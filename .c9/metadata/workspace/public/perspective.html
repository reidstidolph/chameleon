Camera Demo (WASD moves camera)

\n \n

\n FrostFlake creates a default camera object on the game instance.\n The camera can have a position set or position velocity like a Sprite.\n FrostFlake does not currently support camera rotation.\n

\n\n

The frost flake sprites have a random amount \n of parallax so they appear to be positioned at different distances from \n the camera.
